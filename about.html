Our History

Founded in 1985, Oscar Cahn began with a singular vision: to create cues that are not just tools, but extensions of the player's arm. What started in a small workshop in London has grown into a globally recognized brand, trusted by professionals and enthusiasts alike.

We adhere to the strictest standards of quality control. Our timber is naturally seasoned for a minimum of 18 months, ensuring stability and straightness that lasts a lifetime.

The Process

Hand-splicing is the heart of our craft. We select exotic woods like Cocobolo, Tulipwood, and Snakewood for their density and beauty. These are meticulously spliced into the main shaft, creating a solid, vibration-free hit.

Finally, each cue is finished with our secret blend of natural oils and waxes, providing a silky smooth feel that never gets sticky, even under the heat of competition lights.
